# Tax Saving Instruments in India (80C)

The 80C deduction limit is Rs 1.5 lakh per financial year.
ELSS (Equity Linked Savings Scheme) has the shortest lock-in of 3 years among 80C options.
PPF (Public Provident Fund) has a 15-year lock-in with tax-free returns around 7.1%.
NPS (National Pension System) gives additional Rs 50,000 deduction under 80CCD(1B).
Term life insurance premiums also qualify under 80C deductions.
