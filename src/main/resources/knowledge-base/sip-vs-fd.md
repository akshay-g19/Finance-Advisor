# SIP vs Fixed Deposit (FD)

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) allows you to invest a fixed amount monthly into mutual funds.
FDs offer guaranteed returns, currently ranging from 6.5% to 7.5% per annum in Indian banks.
SIPs are market-linked and can give 10-15% returns over long term but carry risk.
FDs are ideal for short-term goals (1-3 years) and risk-averse investors.
SIPs are ideal for long-term goals (5+ years) like retirement or child education.
